Our Story

Born from the
Power of Water

Cascade Coffee was born from a simple observation: the most extraordinary things in nature happen when forces flow together. Just as a waterfall transforms a landscape — carving, smoothing, and creating beauty through persistent motion — our nitro-infused cold brew transforms the coffee experience.

We source single-origin beans from the world's finest growing regions, steep them for 20+ hours in cold, filtered water, and then infuse the result with pure nitrogen. The cascade begins — tiny bubbles rise and fall in a mesmerizing dance, creating a thick, creamy head and a taste so smooth it needs no sugar, no cream, no compromise.

The Experience

A Cascade
in Every Cup

From the moment the tap handle is pulled, the magic begins. Watch as the nitrogen-infused brew cascades through the glass — a waterfall of flavor and texture.

The Source

Single-origin beans sourced from high-altitude farms where mountain streams feed the soil, producing beans with unmatched depth and complexity.

The Steep

Cold-steeped for over 20 hours in pure, filtered water. Low and slow — like a river patiently carving through canyon walls, extracting maximum flavor with zero bitterness.

The Infusion

Pure nitrogen is infused under pressure, creating microbubbles so tiny they're almost invisible — until they cascade. This is where the waterfall begins.

The Cascade

The pour reveals it all — a breathtaking cascade as nitrogen bubbles surge downward then rise, creating a visual waterfall effect and a velvety-smooth, creamy finish.
